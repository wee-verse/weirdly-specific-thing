About Weevil World

Weevil World is an independent collection of quizzes, generators, interactive stories, and — eventually — games, united by an interest in the oddly specific. Nothing here is trying to be productive, educational, or good for you — it's just supposed to be a little fun, and maybe worth sending to a friend with "this is so you" attached.

The collection is nominally curated by Duke Weevilton, a Victorian gentleman weevil of unclear qualifications who insists on cataloguing everything as though it belongs in a museum. We've let him keep the job.

Right now the cabinet holds two small exhibits. More are being built, ranging from quick diversions to things you can properly get lost in — this page will keep describing what actually exists, not what's planned.

Everything on this site works without an account, without a login, and without collecting more about you than it needs to. See the privacy page for the specifics.

Questions, feedback, or an idea for what we should catalogue next? Get in touch.
